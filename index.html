Who I Am

Two Passions,
One Vision

I'm Thilina Kushan Karunathunga — a senior marketing professional and luxury travel entrepreneur based in the Cultural Triangle of Sri Lanka. My life sits at the intersection of two worlds: the high-stakes arena of FMCG marketing, and the extraordinary beauty of Ceylon as experienced through the eyes of international travellers.

At Cargills Ceylon PLC, I've spent over a decade mastering territory management, trade marketing strategy, and channel development across Sri Lanka's most diverse and challenging regions. Away from the boardroom, I founded Lions Ceylonic Tours to share my homeland's wonders with the world — one perfectly crafted itinerary at a time.
